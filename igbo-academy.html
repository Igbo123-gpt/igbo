About Us

Igbo Academy is dedicated to revitalizing and promoting the Igbo language and heritage among children and young adults. Through engaging lessons, cultural immersion, and community events, we aim to reconnect our people with their roots.

Our Classes

We offer beginner to advanced Igbo language classes tailored to different age groups. Our sessions are interactive and fun, incorporating stories, songs, and cultural expressions to aid learning.

  • Children (5–10 years)
  • Teens (11–17 years)
  • Adults (18+)
